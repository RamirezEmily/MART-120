Short Story:

The Voice in the Wall

By the fourth day, a voice answered

But the wall it came from was bare

The voice would speak and give assurance

But all the boy could do was stare

Five days had passed since they met

The boy and the voice were best of friends

Yet they had never seen each other's faces

And the two would talk till each other ends
